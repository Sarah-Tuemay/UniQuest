Network Administrator

Campus Network Center

ScheduleMon–Fri, 9 AM – 5 PM
Duration6 months
Pay Rate70 birr/hr
LocationOn Campus

About the Role

We are seeking a skilled Network Administrator to join our team at the Campus Network Center. The ideal candidate will manage and maintain our network infrastructure, ensuring optimal performance and security.

Responsibilities

  • Monitor network performance and troubleshoot issues
  • Configure and maintain network devices (routers, switches, firewalls)
  • Implement security measures to protect the network
  • Collaborate with IT teams on network-related projects
  • Document network configurations and changes
  • Provide technical support to end-users

Requirements

  • Currently enrolled in Computer Science or related program
  • Strong understanding of networking concepts and protocols
  • Experience with network devices and configurations
  • Knowledge of network security practices
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills
